SHER SHAH SURI TOMB

Sher Shah Suri Tomb is found in the memory of Emperor Sher Shah Suri in 1545 AD. It is a perfect example of Indo-Islamic architecture In India. This place is located in the middle of the artificial lake which makes this place more and more beautiful. Sher Shah Suri was the founder of the Suri Empire in the northern part of India. This tomb is situated in Sasaram in Rohtas district of Bihar. It is the best example of ancient architecture.
