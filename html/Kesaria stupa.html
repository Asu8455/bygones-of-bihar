KESARIA STUPA TOMB

Kesaria Stupa is located in kesaria, East Champaran of Bihar. It is the tallest and largest stupa of Buddha in Bihar. It is one of the tourism places in Bihar. Many people from different cities come to see this place. It is believed that the stupa was established between 200-750 AD during the rule of Raja Chakravarti. Its height is 104 feet. This is the most attractive site you must ever see during a visit to Bihar.
